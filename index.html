Organic Panic


Evidence

The amount of chemicals that reside in Non-Organic food is astounding. Many people don't have access to stores that sell healthy food, or simply cannot afford the more expensive organics.

  • a

    EXPENSIVE

    Simply put, one of the main challenges with eating healthy, organic foods is that it costs more so many people cannot afford it.

  • b

    BENEFITS

    Eating healthy and organic foods has been proven to decrease the number of harmful chemicals found in the body, leading to longer lifespans and happier lives.

    • c

      CHEMICALS

      A growing number of medical researchers are decisively saying that glyphosate, the primary chemical in Roundup and other herbicides, is collecting in our non-organic and unhealthy foods and may be the main cause of most major illnesses.

    • d

      DANGEROUS

      Non-organic foods have much higher levels of chemicals and pesticides in them, which when eaten will be introduced into our bloodstream and may cause extreme damage to our bodies. This issue is a pressing matter for America, because disease and illness, particularly for the poor, are reaching epidemic proportions.

Solutions

Possible solutions or ideas to help fix this issue that are being actively pursued or have been thought out.

  • Current

    Incentivize growing of Organic or Healthy foods.

    Legislation

    By creating legislation to incentivize the sale and growth of healthy or organic foods, farmers would be more inclined to grow organic and healthy foods, leading to a greater supply which would drive the prices of these foods down.s

  • Current

    Spread the word about this issue to raise awareness

    Social

    By increasing public awareness through social media and other outlets, the public interest in this issue will increase exponentially which in turn will force governments to push legislation that makes lasting end effective changes.

  • Future

    Educate communities on the importance of this issue

    Education

    One of the big problems that the spread of healthy/organic foods faces is a lack of education. In a study titled, “Consumer behavior and purchase intention for organic food,” the authors concluded that “Stores should develop effective marketing program and strategies to influence consumers positively. They can emphasize the health benefits and quality of organic food. They can make these products easily available to attract consumers to buy organic food.” By increasing public knowledge of how much healthier these foods are, there will be a push to eat them more often.

  • Future

    Limit the use of dangerous chemicals on or near food crops

    Legislation

    Dangerous chemicals such as glyphosate are being used in exretme amounts on the crops we eat, and in turn are collecting in our bloodstreams. Legislatioon needs to be drafted that will keep these chemicals away from our food or decrease the use on our crops.

Data

2. 41.0% of the american population will be diagnosed with cancer in their lifetime. The WHO stated that if people exersise more and eat healthier, up to 40 percent of cancer could be prevented.

3. Only 57.7% of Americans regularly consume five servings of vegetables more than four days per week.

4. 80.4% of all the packaged foods sold within the United States contain chemicals outlawed in other parts of the world.
